ABOUT

I'm Ruth Larmore, a writer and visual artist living in the Northeastern US. I was born in Las Vegas and moved east to learn about organic regenerative farming. Working on farms has been an incredible experience, and now I am getting back into other creative work. Currently, I'm working on a series of vignettes about the confusions of intimacy.
My poetry has been published in BAG Magazine and Storm of Blue Press.

I am currently working as a freelance writer, editor/proofreader, and graphic artist. Feel free to contact me with any inquiries!
